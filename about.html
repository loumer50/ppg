About Us

Professional Parking Group is a leader and professional in the car parking industry and we are optimistic that we can bring to you and your organization the wealth of knowledge, experience and provide the solution toward the successful operation of your car parking facility.
Professional Parking Group understands very well that an integral balance has to be maintained in the aspect of increasing revenue and maintaining an optimum customer service level in every car park that we operate.

Professional Parking's objective and purpose is to provide the lessors whom we serve with our brand of value-added service that enhances the car park revenue in order to attain the maximum yield with the goal of providing "A Delightful Parking Experience" to all our owners/landlords and our parking customers.

Providing the highest value to shareholders and stakeholders, the best stage to employees and maximum satisfaction to customers.

To build up a national brand that is respected and preferred, and to be Number 1 in the car parking industry in the country.

Professional Parking Group has also established itself as an innovator in the parking industry, as the company continuously adapts the best practices and concepts in the international parking industry. In addition, its core asset, its people study each car park environment intimately and passionately, determining operational challenges that are unique to each site, and present solutions that will make an over-all positive impact on the car park’s design, operations, and revenue generation.
